Week 07

Computer-Controlled Machining

Group assignment

  • test runout, alignment, speeds, feeds, and toolpaths for your machine

Individual assignment

  • Make something big (on a CNC machine).

Learning outcomes:

  • Demonstrate 2D design development for CNC production
  • Describe workflows for CNC production

Have you:

  • Explained how you made your files for machining (2D or 3D)
  • Shown how you made something BIG (setting up the machine, using fixings, testing joints, adjusting feeds and speeds, depth of cut etc)
  • Described problems and how you fixed them
  • Included your design files and ‘hero shot’ photos of final object

Tools used

  • large self made CNC

Software Used

  • Solid Works
  • HSM
  • Mach3

This is part of my final Project, The Large CNC The idea I have in mind was to have all the electronics inside a box that could be adapted with time with different "windows" that could be exchanged as I change the equipment without having to change everything.
